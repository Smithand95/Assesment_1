What is:C.R.A.B

C.R.A.B is a nonprofit organisation set up by myself and a number of other co-founders, together we formed the Crustaceans Rights Activists Bureau!, the organisation was originally founded in response to culling of the russian king crab, however our remit has expanded to focusing on the development of sustainable crustacean farming methods, that take into account their ecological impact to other more protected species. we are not against responsible and sustainable farming of crustaceans, some species are delicious and under no threat of extinction. Our key aims involve educating the public about responsible crabbing/lobster fishing, increasing awareness of the more endangered species, and to discourage culling, encouraging productive usage of that meat.
